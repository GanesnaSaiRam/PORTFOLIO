Academic Counseling Management System

Introduction:

This project “ACADEMIC COUNSELING MANAGEMENT SYSTEM” is designed to maintain the information regarding the information about the various top universities and their affiliated colleges at the time of counseling. It provides the details with the help of their cut-off ranks in various entrance exams.

The main purpose of this project is to know about the eligibility criteria of getting admissions into the respective colleges and universities. A student can also get information regarding how many seats are available in the institutions and the fee for the respective courses. It helps a student for selecting the branch in which they are interested.

Project Overview:

The "ACADEMIC COUNSELING MANAGEMENT SYSTEM" is aimed at organizing and maintaining comprehensive information about top universities and their affiliated colleges during counseling periods. It facilitates access to details such as cut-off ranks in various entrance exams, enabling students to understand eligibility criteria for admissions.

Additionally, the system provides information on available seats in institutions and the corresponding fees for respective courses. Its primary objective is to assist students in selecting their preferred branch of study by offering valuable insights into the admissions process. Through this project, students can make informed decisions regarding their educational journey.

Summary:

The "Academic Counseling Management System" was developed to address manual inefficiencies faced by institutes while maintaining a database for future reference. Flow diagrams were used to represent system processes, guiding the design of the database with user consultation. Various programs were created to operate on this database. User-friendly forms and reports were developed to efficiently manage system functionality. The software is designed for ease of use, allowing users to interact with minimal training. Overall, the project was a valuable learning experience.
